About Me

I'm Jordan, and I graduated from University of Washington with a Bachelor's degree in Meteorology. I love learning and problem solving, especially when it comes to building web apps.

First Exposure

My first exposure to coding was during my freshman at UW, where I took two Java classes. My favorite part about the courses was practicing many problems in order to study for exams, especially when they got challenging and started to involve Linked Lists and Binary Trees.

3-4 years later, I took a Web Development class, while also working with MATLAB in my Applied Math (AMATH) classes. I wanted to have a career in Software Development when I realized that the Shooting method I learned in AMATH was very similar to the the Binary Search algorithm.
